Next-Gen Intelligent Technologies in Computing and Communication
[NITCom-2024]

Objectives

The purpose of the NITCom-2023 conference is to promote research in technologies of the future and to provide a common platform for collaboration and exchange of valuable research, contributions, opinions & innovations for renowned professionals, researchers, academicians, industrialists, and scholars practitioners, scientists, and engineers in affiliated disciplines.

Scope

Advancement in technology has made the world move at a faster pace and intelligence in the next-gen technologies has made this move more smartly. Technological trends are changing very fast, especially in computing and communication. The scope of the conference is to encourage participation in next-gen trends like Internet of Things (IoT), Blockchain, Cyber-Physical Systems, Digital Transformation, Metaverse, Smart Cities, Machine to Machine etc. Artificial Intelligence and Data Science is an ongoing era and also foresee this as long term future of the technology including Data Analytics, Deep Learning, Neural Networks, Fuzzy Logic, Expert Systems, Agents and Multi-agent Systems, etc. Now the field of computing has shifted towards High-Performance Computing and moving towards Quantum Computing. Communication is the main ingredient of today’s technical world; without the term communication, next-gen technologies are not even feasible to discuss. Regress areas from communication which has been addressed are Connected Machines, Satellite Communication Systems, 4G/5G/6G Network Evolutions and Open Spectrum Solutions. The last but most important need of all the times of technologies is “security”. Technologies of the future also put challenges in front of security experts that were not previously seen and the scenario is changing at a rapid pace. etc. The organizers aim to motivate and encourage the researchers in the relevant domain to contribute by submitting their original articles that illustrate research results, projects, surveying works and industrial experiences that describe significant advances in the areas of computing and communication.
